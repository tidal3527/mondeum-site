Mondeum: A Model for a Cross-Border Multi-Fiat Stablecoin Exchange

TLDR: This post outlines a new model for cross-border stablecoin exchange—one that routes all trades through a global basket token called Mondeum, backed by fiat currency baskets from around the world. It introduces a three-layered liquidity system to separate token risk, treasury risk and global routing. The result: lower slippage, better capital efficiency and a more regulator-friendly foundation for the next phase of stablecoin adoption. We walk through the rationale, architecture and rollout roadmap.
