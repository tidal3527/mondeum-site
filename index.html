Mondeum

A Smarter Routing Layer for Stablecoins

Mondeum is a global stablecoin infrastructure that routes all swaps through a neutral basket token. It separates token, currency and global risk across three layers—making stablecoin FX cheaper, cleaner and easier to scale.
